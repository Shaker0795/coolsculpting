CoolSculpting

CAN YOU PINCH AN INCH? OR TWO? OR THREE?

CoolSculpting has the life-changing technology to get back the body you deserve without needles, downtime or surgery.

Developed by renowned Harvard University scientists Dieter Manstein, MD and R. Rox Anderson, MD, CoolSculpting is a revolutionary FDA-cleared contouring treatment that freezes stubborn fat, which is then naturally eliminated from your body. Cryolipolysis, the science behind CoolSculpting, was founded on the observation that cold can selectively affect fat cells and gradually destroy them.


After this no-needles, no-downtime treatment, results are undeniable and the impact is immeasurable. With CoolSculpting you can put the “little” back in your black dress!

How It Works

Real Patient Photo Gallery

Just a small sampling of why our patients are so pleased.

CoolSculpting is a one-hour treatment that safely delivers precisely-controlled cooling to gently and effectively target fat cells. The treated fat cells are crystallized (frozen), then die. Over time, your body naturally processes the fat and eliminates it, leaving a more sculpted you. Your results can be seen as early as three weeks after your treatment, with the most dramatic results after one to three months.
